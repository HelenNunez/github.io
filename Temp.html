This scatter diagram shows how the temperature raises as the latitude gets closer to latitude zero. As expected the temperatures are higher around the equator.

Temperatures around the equator get close to the 90 degrees Farhenheit and interestingly, between the latitudes of 60 and 80 the temperatures drop drammatically. Ideal conditions may be between 60 and 80 degrees, and there is a broad range of latitudes around the globe that have those temperatures.
